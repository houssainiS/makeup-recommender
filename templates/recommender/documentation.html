BeautyAI Documentation

Complete guide to setting up and using your Shopify app

1App Installation

  • After installing the app, you are redirected to the "App Installed Successfully" page.
  • On this page, you will see two buttons:
    • Create Page & Navigation Link: Automatically creates a dedicated page in your store and adds a nav bar link for the app.
    • Open Theme Editor (Deep Link): Opens the Shopify theme editor directly on the page created for the app.

2Metafield Setup

1The app automatically creates a metafield called usage duration (in days) for products.

  • Merchants can fill in this metafield for products they want to track usage for.
  • After a customer purchases a product with this metafield, they will receive a notification email when the duration ends.

3Customer Notifications

  • When a customer purchases a product with a usage duration, the app sends an email to notify them when the duration ends.
  • Emails are sent only for this purpose and are not used for marketing.

4App Behavior Summary

Face Analysis
Customers can upload a photo, which is analyzed temporarily by the backend for beauty tips.
Data Handling
Photos are never stored; only IP/device info and email notifications are retained temporarily.
Navigation & Theme
Merchants can create and edit pages with our app using provided buttons.

5Security & Compliance

  • App uses HTTPS, Django sessions, CSRF protection, and limited access to logs.
  • Complies with GDPR, including explicit consent for photo analysis and handling of IP/email info.

Security Features: All data transfers are encrypted, sessions are handled securely, and access to logs is strictly limited.
